About This Project

Last updated:

Qvideo Downloader is a free Application that helps you fetch a downloadable link from a video or playlist URL. It’s built to be fast, simple, and clean—no accounts, no ads, and no cookies.

What We Believe

  • Free & non-monetized: You can use the app without paying. Donations are optional and never required.
  • Privacy first: We don’t use cookies (including third-party cookies), trackers, analytics beacons, or push notifications.
  • Minimal footprint: Lightweight UI, no pop-ups, no clutter.

How It Works

  1. Paste a full video or playlist URL in the input box.
  2. Pick a format or quality.
  3. Click Download. Your links appear under the button. When the button unlocks, click to save.

Tip: Larger videos can take a moment to prepare; keep the tab open until the link is ready.

What We Don’t Do

  • No ads, no third-party cookies, no tracking.
  • No user accounts and no personal data collection.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of user data—ever.
